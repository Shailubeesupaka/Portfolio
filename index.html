Artifact 4 · Case Study

ML vs DL

This case study compares machine learning and deep learning through two practical examples: Zillow-style home value prediction and Apple Face ID recognition.

  • Used Zestimate-style pricing as an example of structured data where ML is easier to explain and deploy
  • Used Face ID-style image recognition to show why DL performs better on high-dimensional visual data
  • Highlighted that the better approach depends on problem type, data structure, and model transparency
  • Connected ML with tabular inputs such as size, location, and past tax records
  • Connected DL with visual inputs where millions of pixels must be interpreted together
  • Emphasized that accuracy, cost, and explainability all shape the right choice
  • Presented home valuation as a clear example of ML learning from structured features
  • Presented Face ID as a clear example of DL learning patterns from complex image data
